### CraftLog
**CraftLog** is a Claude skill that acts as your virtual engineering manager. Each week, say "run CraftLog" and it pulls your actual PRs, tickets, and Slack threads to generate a team-facing status post and a private career growth reflection — with evidence-backed scores, gap analysis, and concrete next steps toward your next level. First run kicks off a one-time setup (~17 questions about your tools, level, and company values), then it remembers everything and tracks your progress week over week in Google Drive. No padding, no guessing — just what you shipped, what it means, and what to do next.
